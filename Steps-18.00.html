Adding Map Visual 3 (Work by state): 18

Steps:

      1.  Click on an empty space on the canvas.

2.     From the visualizations pane, select 'Shape map'.

3.     Configure the Columns and Measures as follows:

    • Location: Work_State
    • Color Saturation: Apps per Mth

4.     Set tooltips to display the following fields: "AR", "AR YoY%", "Wage YoY%", "Apps per Yr", and "Apps per Mth YoY%".

5.     Adjust the visual settings:

    • Fill colors: Colors (Gradient 'On', Min(#B0CCF4), Center (#414FB1), Max(#0E1A77))
    • Blank areas: Show blank areas 'On', Color(#DBDEF1)
    • Border: Color(#12239E), Width(1)

6.     In the General properties:

    • Size: Height(327), Width(360)
    • Position: Horizontal(205), Vertical(393)

7.     Configure the Title:

    • Title 'On'
    • Text: 'Work by State'
    • Font: DIN, 14
    • Text Color: #FFFFFF
    • Background color: #414FB1
    • Center alignment
    • Text wrap 'On'

8.     Apply Effects:

    • Background 'On', Color(#EAEBF7), Transparency: 70%
    • Visual border 'On', Color(#B2B8E9)
