Transforming the data in power Query Editor 9

Steps:  

  • 1.     In the Power Query Editor window, locate and select the "Transform Data" option.
  • 2.     Locate and right-click on the "Decision_Date" column. And choose the “Duplicate Column” option.
  • 3.     Locate "Decision_Date Copy" Column and right click on it and choose “transform” and “Year” option to extract the year from it.

  • 4.     Rename the "Decision_Date Copy" to "Year" by right click on the column header.
