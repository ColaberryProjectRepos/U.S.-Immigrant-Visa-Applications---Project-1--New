Creating the 'Refresh Date' and 'Calendar' table 10

Steps: Creating the 'Refresh Date'



Steps:

1.     Click "Transform Data" on the Power BI Desktop ribbon.

2.     Select "Blank query" from the "New Source" button dropdown and click "Connect".

3.     In the properties, name the query as 'Refresh Date'.

4.     In the DAX formula bar, enter ' =DateTime.LocalNow()'.

5.     Click "Convert to Table" button.

6.     Rename the column to 'Date Time' by right-clicking on the column header.

7.     Click "Close & Apply" to apply the changes and save the project.

Steps: Creating the 'Calendar' table


Steps:

1.     Open Power BI Desktop and navigate to the 'Modeling' tab.

2.     Click on the 'New Table' button to create a new table.

3.     In the DAX formula bar, paste the following formula:

 

Calendar = ADDCOLUMNS (CALENDARAUTO(),

"Year", Year([Date]),

"Month Number", Month([Date]),

"Month Name Short", FORMAT([Date],"MMM"))

 4.     Press the checkmark icon or hit Enter to validate the formula. And save your Power BI project.
