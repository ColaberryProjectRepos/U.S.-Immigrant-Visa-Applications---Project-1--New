You can download the updated dataset (US_Perm_Visas) and save it on your computer from the zip file attached. The updated dataset has been modified to remove unnecessary data, including Personally Identifiable Information (PII), from the source file.

 Steps:

1.     Go to Power BI Desktop.

2.     Click on the "Get Data" button on the home tab in Power BI Desktop.

3.     Choose Excel workbook and connect.

4.     It will direct you to saved files, choose the ‘US_Perm_Visas’ file and click on Open.

5.     Once the data is successfully retrieved, you will see a preview of the data in power BI Desktop, click on Transform Data.
