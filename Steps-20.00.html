Adding Global Map Visual 5 (Apps by Country) 20

Steps:

1.     Create a new "Map" visual and title it "Apps by Country." Position this visual in the bottom right box.

2.     From the "Main Dataset," drag and drop "Country of Citizenship" into the "Location" tab of the chart. Then, drag "Apps by Mth" into the "Size" tab of the chart.

3.     Add the following fields to the "Tooltips" tab of the chart: "AR," "AR YoY%," "Apps per Mth YoY," "App per Yr," and "Wage YoY%."

4.     Modify the colors of the bubbles according to the provided GIF.

5.     Adjust the bubble size to 10.
